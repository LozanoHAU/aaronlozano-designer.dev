She's Cakes

Tools & Technologies
Express.js Node.js Figma REST API HTML/CSS JavaScript

She's Cakes is a sweet local spot where they whip up delicious, handmade cakes perfect for any celebration. However, since they didn't have a website yet, customers needed give them a call or shoot them an email to discuss their design and place an order directly.

This is why I made this prototype web application as a proof-of-concept for the business. It demonstrates the core functionality of a small e-commerce platform; customers can browse a catalog of cakes, submit custom order requests, and send contact inquiries, while the bakery owner can access a basic admin dashboard to view incoming orders and messages. A simple one-time-token-protected email mockup page is also included to simulate the idea of replying to inquiries.
